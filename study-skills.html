📝 Study Skills

Strong study skills form the foundation of long-term academic success. I help students develop effective learning habits that improve understanding, retention, and independent learning.

What I help with:

✔ Effective note-taking techniques

✔ Time management and organisation

✔ Creating realistic study schedules

✔ Revision strategies that work

✔ Goal setting and progress tracking

✔ Building independent learning habits

Who it's for: Students in Year 6–12 who want to improve their learning habits, become more organised, and study more effectively.

Approach: Practical, personalised, and focused on creating sustainable habits that support long-term academic growth.

Outcome: Better organisation, increased confidence, improved learning efficiency, and stronger academic performance.
